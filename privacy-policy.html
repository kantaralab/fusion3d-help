Privacy Policy

Fusion 3D Template

Data Collection

Fusion3D Template does not collect, store, or process any personal data of merchants or customers.

The template operates entirely within the Ecwid (by Lightspeed) platform and uses only publicly available storefront data required to render visual and interactive sections.

Data Sharing

No personal information, order data, or customer data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

Security

All assets are delivered via secure HTTPS connections.

What We Don't Do

The template does not manage:

  • Authentication
  • Payments
  • Customer accounts
  • Billing
